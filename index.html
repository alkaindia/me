I'm Alka

Greetings! Welcome to my page!
I am Alka Yadav, currently serving as an Assistant Professor - Senior Scale at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dehradun, India and as Manager – Learning at the Sabudh Foundation, an NGO dedicated to data-driven social impact. I hold a Ph.D. from Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), New Delhi, in collaboration with the Chennai Mathematical Institute (CMI), India. My research centers on complex systems, with a strong emphasis on climate change and environmental dynamics. As a physicist turned data scientist, I specialize in advanced data-driven methodologies—ranging from time-series analysis and Bayesian inference to machine learning and AI—to understand and address environmental challenges. Passionate about the social impact of science, I lead and mentor data science initiatives for social good, focusing on ethical, inclusive, and empowering practices. I welcome interdisciplinary collaborations, research partnerships, and opportunities that bridge data science with real-world impact—particularly in the domains of climate, environment, and social good. Feel free to connect!
