I'm Alka

I hold a Ph.D. from Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi, in collaboration with the Chennai Mathematical Institute, India . My research focuses on complex systems, with a particular emphasis on climate change and environmental systems. I am passionate about exploring the intricate dynamics of these systems to better understand and address the pressing challenges posed by environmental transformations. Additionally, I am deeply committed to ethical and empowering data science practices, recognizing their vital role in advancing scientific understanding and societal well-being.
